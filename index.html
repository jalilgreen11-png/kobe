About the Masjid

A legacy of authentic knowledge in Los Angeles

For more than three decades, Masjid Al-Mumin has served the Muslims of Los Angeles as a center of prayer, da'wah, and learning. Our masjid was shepherded for over twenty years by the late Sheikh Abu Mujaahid Fareed Abdullah — may Allah have mercy on him — whose quiet scholarship, humility, and adherence to the Sunnah still shape who we are.

Today we continue that path: teaching Qur'an, Arabic, Tawheed, Fiqh, and Hadith; raising the next generation through our weekend Islamic school; and welcoming every sincere seeker through our doors.

In Memory

Sheikh Abu Mujaahid Fareed Abdullah

رحمه الله

Our beloved Sheikh served as imam of Masjid Al-Mumin for over twenty years. A graduate of the Islamic University of Imam Muhammad Ibn Saud, a student of Sheikh Ibn Baz and Sheikh Ibn Uthaymeen, and a companion of Sheikh Muqbil al-Wadi'i — may Allah have mercy on them all.

His method was to follow the Qur'an and Sunnah with the understanding of the Salaf. His humility, knowledge, and love for the Sunnah left a mark on all who met him.
